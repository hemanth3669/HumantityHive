Explore Landmark Legal Cases

Understanding past legal cases helps shape the future of justice. This portal provides in-depth analysis of major cases that have influenced laws and societal norms. Learn about the incidents, judicial proceedings, and the reforms they triggered.

Case Study 1: Nirbhaya Case

The 2012 Nirbhaya case involved a brutal crime that led to significant legal reforms in India, including stricter laws for crimes against women and the establishment of fast-track courts.

Case Study 2: Vishaka Guidelines

The Vishaka case was a landmark judgment that led to the formation of laws against sexual harassment in workplaces, shaping gender equality in India.
