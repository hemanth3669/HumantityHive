Legal Insights into Workplace Harassment Laws in India

Introduction

The **Vishaka Guidelines** were established by the **Supreme Court of India** in **1997** in response to the **Bhanwari Devi case**, where a woman was gang-raped as an act of revenge for stopping child marriages.

Case Overview

**Case Name:** Vishaka & Others v. State of Rajasthan (1997)

**Victim:** Bhanwari Devi, a social worker from Rajasthan

**Crime:** Gang-rape in retaliation for social activism

**Judgment:** Supreme Court established the **Vishaka Guidelines** to prevent workplace harassment.
