Introduction to the Motor Vehicle Act

The Motor Vehicle Act is an important set of laws that govern the registration, licensing, regulation, and usage of vehicles on public roads. It ensures that vehicles are used safely, fairly, and in compliance with all traffic and environmental regulations.
